# IEBC Voter Guidance Source Notes

Source: IEBC public voter education and registration-centre guidance.

Voters should confirm registration details and registration or polling centre information through official IEBC channels.

The Sauti ya Mwananchi demo uses public IEBC registration-centre data. Registration centres become polling centres during elections, but users should confirm their final official polling station through IEBC before election day.

A voter does not need a political party membership card to vote. Voting depends on official voter registration, not party membership.

Voters should not share ID numbers, passport numbers, dates of birth, or private voter registration details with unofficial agents.
